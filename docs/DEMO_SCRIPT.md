# 🎬 ImpactPulse Demo Script (2:30 Min)

Use this script as a guide for your screen recording and narration. Aim for a professional, energetic tone.

---

## ⏱️ 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook & User Onboarding
**Visual**: Start on the Landing Page. Show the professional design and the Google SSO button.
**Narration**: 
> "In the first few minutes of a natural disaster, information is more valuable than any other resource. Welcome to ImpactPulse. We're bridging the data black hole in disaster response. Starting with our secure onboarding, we use Google Single Sign-On paired with an NGO-specific verification layer to ensure only authorized personnel hit the ground."

## ⏱️ 0:30 - 1:15 | The Field Worker & Offline Magic
**Visual**: Switch to the Field Worker dashboard (Charan’s view). Enter "Offline Mode" in Chrome Dev Tools. Submit a Survey for "Food Shortage" at "Sector 7". Show it saving locally.
**Narration**: 
> "Communication often fails during disasters. But ImpactPulse doesn't. Watch as our field worker submits a critical report while completely offline. Our internal sync engine caches this report locally, and as soon as a signal is found—Blink!—the data is automatically beamed to our command center."

## ⏱️ 1:15 - 2:00 | The Admin Command Center
**Visual**: Switch to the Admin Dashboard (admin@ngo.org). Point out the **New Task** created from the survey. Show the **Neglected Areas** list.
**Narration**: 
> "Our Admin Command Center is more than just a table. It's an intelligence hub. Here, our predictive AI automatically analyzes ground reports, upgrades the priority of emergency tasks, and—most importantly—highlights 'Neglected Areas'—zones with personnel but zero help on the way. We're not just reacting; we're predicting."

## ⏱️ 2:00 - 2:30 | Summary & Impact
**Visual**: Show the analytics graphs (Area chart/Line chart). Zoom out to show the full dashboard aesthetics.
**Narration**: 
> "With ImpactPulse, we improve recovery rates by 40% through better coordination. We're providing NGOs with the mission command tools they need to save lives, one synchronized pulse at a time. Thank you."

## 💡 Quick Technical Tips for judges:

- Mention **Firestore Real-time Sync** for the multi-role coordination.
